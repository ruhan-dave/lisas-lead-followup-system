# Brand Guidelines for My Address Number

## Company Overview

[My Address Number](https://www.myaddressnumber.com/) is a North American-based premium home decor company that specializes in modern, architectural-grade address numbers and plaques for residential and commercial properties. We help homeowners elevate their curb appeal by bridging the gap between high-end design and DIY simplicity, with a proven track record of over 30,000 satisfied clients worldwide.

## Brand Voice

  - **Professional yet approachable**: Expert-level engineering delivered with neighborly warmth.
  - **Warm and friendly**: Building connection and trust through a "Share the ❤️" community.
  - **Expert and knowledgeable**: Demonstrating deep understanding of visibility, alignment, and material science.
  - **Customer-focused**: Obsessed with making the user experience "so easy, a kid can do it".
  - **Quality-conscious**: Focused on high standards of North American manufacturing and fast, reliable delivery.

## Common Customer Pain Points

  - **Installation Anxiety**: Fear of crooked numbers or not having the right tools for a professional look.
  - **Poor Nighttime Visibility**: Difficulty for visitors or delivery drivers to find the home after dark.
  - **Dated Aesthetics**: Traditional hardware that clashes with modern or minimalist architecture.
  - **Durability Concerns**: Hardware that rusts, fades, or degrades due to harsh weather.

## How We Help

## Tone Guidelines

  - **Use "we" and "you"**: Focus on our shared goal of making your home look its best.
  - **Direct and Clear**: Focus on the "Easy Like 1-2-3" logic.
  - **Avoid Jargon**: Explain technical benefits (like the "floating effect") in simple, visual terms.
  - **Benefit-Oriented**: Don't just list the level; mention the "perfectly straight result every time".

## Email Style

  - **Personal and Conversational**: "We've literally thought of everything so you don't have to".
  - **Product-Specific**: Tailor recommendations based on the user's home style (e.g., [Modern](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.myaddressnumber.com/collections/address-plaques/products/modern) or [Prestige](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.myaddressnumber.com/products/black-prestige)).
  - **Concise and Scannable**: Use short, plain paragraphs or numbered lists to emphasize that "everything comes in the box". Avoid asterisk (*) or dash (-) bullet points.
  - **Action-Oriented**: End with a call to "Explore" or "Watch how easy it is".
